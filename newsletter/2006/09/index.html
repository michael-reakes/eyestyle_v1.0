Blinde Joins EYESTYLE.COM.AU

Richard Walker began in South Florida seven years ago where he created his first BLINDE collection by hand sculpting frames. In a crowded marketplace of often-indistinguishable sunglasses, his designs have a distinct edge. The difference is dimensional. Walker conceives of sunglasses as sculpture in the round, a 3D process, whereas the most common method is to sketch the frames flat on a piece of paper, strictly 2D.

Passion defines his lifestyle and provides inspiration for his design aesthetic. The sleek lines and smooth curves of his BLINDE sunglass designs have become a staple in the wardrobes of top fashion trendsetters, celebrities, and in the world of extreme sports.

In 1998, Richard Walker was given the opportunity to design the sunglasses for the principal characters in THE MATRIX, and the quintessential hero sunglass for Angelina Jolie in TOMB RAIDER. He returned to THE MATRIX as the sunglass designer for the sequel films THE MATRIX Reloaded and MATRIX Revolutions.

Like Keanu and Angelina, you will be sure to make an impact with these edgey frames.
